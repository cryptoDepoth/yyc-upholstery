Why Pet Stains Are Tricky

Pet urine contains uric acid crystals that bind to fabric fibers. Regular cleaners mask the smell temporarily, but the crystals remain — and when humidity rises, the odor returns. That's why DIY solutions often fail.

The YYC 3-Step Method

  1. Extract — We remove as much liquid as possible using industrial wet/dry extraction.
  2. Enzyme Treatment — Our eco-friendly enzyme solution breaks down uric acid at the molecular level. Safe for kids, pets, and allergy sufferers.
  3. Steam & Dry — Hot water extraction sanitizes the fabric, followed by rapid drying (24 hours max).
