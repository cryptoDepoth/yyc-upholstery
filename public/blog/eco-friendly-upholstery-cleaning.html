What Makes Our Cleaning "Eco-Friendly"?

  • Plant-based surfactants — Derived from coconut and corn, not petroleum.
  • No VOCs — Volatile Organic Compounds can trigger asthma and allergies. We use zero-VOC formulas.
  • Biodegradable — Our wastewater won't harm Calgary's rivers or soil.
  • Safe for babies — pH-neutral, dermatologist-tested, fragrance-free options available.

The Hidden Danger of Traditional Cleaners

Many "professional grade" upholstery cleaners contain:

  • Perchloroethylene (PERC) — linked to neurological issues
  • Naphthalene — suspected carcinogen
  • Butoxyethanol — irritates skin and lungs

These chemicals can linger in fabric for days or weeks after cleaning. With our method, your sofa is safe to use within 4–6 hours.

Why Calgary's Hard Water Matters

Calgary has moderately hard water (180–220 ppm calcium). Hard water reduces soap effectiveness and leaves mineral residue. We use softened water + chelating agents for optimal cleaning without buildup.
