Calgary Homeowner's Guide to Upholstery Care

Calgary's dry climate and frequent temperature changes mean your furniture needs extra care:

  • Steam clean your sofa every 6–12 months
  • Run a dehumidifier in dusty seasons (spring/fall)
  • Protect light-colored fabrics with UV-blocking window film
  • Rotate cushions monthly to prevent uneven wear
